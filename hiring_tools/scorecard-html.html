Hiring Recommendation

Proceed with Caution

Sarah is a solid candidate with good technical skills and a track record of delivering results. However, the team leadership concerns need to be addressed before making a final decision.

Recommended Next Steps:

  1. Conduct reference checks specifically focused on team leadership and matrix management
  2. Arrange panel interview with peer managers to assess cultural fit
  3. Consider a case study presentation on managing distributed teams
  4. Discuss detailed development plan for first 90 days if hired
  5. Potentially negotiate a lower starting level with clear path to target role
