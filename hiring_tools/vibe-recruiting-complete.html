4 Push-Pull Messaging

▼

The Framework

  1. Subject Line: Your [X project] caught my eye - 30% leap?
  2. Pull: Compelling problem matching their work
  3. Push: "It's not for everyone..."
  4. Invite: "If you'd want to hear more..."
  5. Trust Signal: Real name and context

Explorer Message Template

Subject: Your work at [Company] caught my eye - could this be your next 30% leap? Hi Taylor, I came across the project you ran on [specific achievement] - impressive scale and real impact. We're tackling something similar but messier: a multi-source insights overhaul that links voice-of-customer to actual product pivots. It's not for everyone - high visibility, lots of ambiguity, and we need someone who loves connecting data to real business moves. If you'd ever want to hear how this could stretch your influence 30% beyond where you are now, I'd love 15 minutes to share the details. No pressure if timing's off. Best, Jordan - Senior Recruiter, Atlas Consumer Products

5 The Career Move Call

▼

7-Point Flow

Step Script
1. Warm Rapport "Your [project] caught my attention..."
2. Universal Yes "Would you explore something clearly superior?"
3. 30% Frame "This should stretch you 30% beyond current"
4. Test Results "Tell me about your biggest comparable project"
5. Career Logic "Why did you move from X to Y?"
6. Money Talk "Let's see if the work is a step up first"
7. Conditional Yes "If this is a true career move, would you proceed?"
