How AI Agents Build Your Referral Network

  1. Scan Your 1st Degree Connections: AI agents analyze your existing LinkedIn network to identify people who likely know candidates for your target role
  2. Categorize Relationships: Automatically classify connections into the 5 universal categories based on their relationship to your ideal candidate
  3. Analyze Their Networks: AI examines the connections of your 1st-degree contacts to find potential candidates who have worked with them
  4. Score & Prioritize: AI scores potential referral sources based on:
    • Relevance of their role to your target position
    • Strength of their network in the target domain
    • Quality of mutual connections
    • Recent interaction patterns
  5. Generate Outreach Templates: AI creates personalized messages for each connection type, referencing shared context
  6. Track & Optimize: Monitor response rates and referral quality to continuously improve targeting
