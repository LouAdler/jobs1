Key Implementation Insights

The 30% Solution Principle

A true career move should provide at least 30% non-monetary increase through stretch assignments, impact, growth opportunities, and satisfaction - even if the salary increase is modest.

Win-Win Hiring Outcomes

When candidates score opportunities using this framework, over 90% remain enthusiastic about their job after the first year - creating true Win-Win Hiring outcomes.

Beyond Transactional Hiring

Most hiring processes are too transactional. Both companies and candidates must think long-term about career integration and mutual success, not just immediate fit.

The Information Gap Problem

Companies often make offers without giving candidates a complete picture of the role, leading to misaligned expectations and high turnover rates.

Candidate Due Diligence

Before accepting any offer, ask hiring managers to describe challenges, performance metrics, and meet potential peers to gather comprehensive information.

Benchmark Against Your Best Role

Score your best previous job using this framework as a benchmark. Don't accept anything that scores significantly lower, regardless of compensation.

Your Career is Your Most Valuable Asset

Time is finite. Don't gamble with career decisions based on short-term thinking. Use this framework to make informed, strategic career moves that compound over time.
