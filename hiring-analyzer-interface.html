What This Tool Does

This tool generates a customized prompt for Claude to analyze any HR Tech solution against the principle of Win-Win Hiring - the belief that true hiring success goes beyond filling roles efficiently to achieving stronger post-hire performance for BOTH the organization and the new employee.

Win-Win Hiring means:

  • Hiring managers make better long-term team-building decisions
  • Candidates make informed career moves that accelerate their growth
  • Success is measured by mutual satisfaction after one year, not just hire completion
  • Any HR tech must be truly bidirectional - serving both parties equally

The analysis introduces two critical metrics:

NEX (New Employee Experience): Tracks performance, satisfaction, and engagement throughout the first year

MLS (Management Leadership Score): Measures how well hiring managers attract, build, develop, coach, and manage their teams
