Role Overview and Key Challenges

As a Senior VDC Engineer at VIATechnik, you will play a key role in applying advanced VDC processes across all stages of major construction projects, from concept through design, construction, and facility operations. You will serve as a client-facing consultant while managing VDC project execution, ensuring digital workflows deliver tangible improvements in constructability, efficiency, and cost management. Within the first year, you will independently deliver multiple BIM/VDC assignments, lead trade coordination efforts, and serve as a trusted advisor to clients on digital construction best practices. The role requires exceptional organizational skills, technical expertise in model coordination, and a passion for innovation in BIM/VDC, including applying AI and automation to accelerate outcomes.

Key Challenges You'll Own

Join us to tackle these game-changing initiatives:

Lead Game-Changing Projects
Take ownership of first 12 months high-profile initiatives that reshape how construction gets done. You'll lead the execution of at least three major bim/vdc projects from concept through construction, focusing on trade coordination, clash detection, and constructability improvements.
Become a Trusted Industry Advisor
Build strategic relationships with Fortune 500 clients and industry leaders. Your insights will directly influence major decisions and shape project success.
Orchestrate Global Innovation
Unite teams across continents to deliver flawless execution. Achieve at least 95 performance while pioneering digital collaboration.

Why This Role Matters

Purpose: This role offers the opportunity to shape digital construction practices on iconic projects such as airports, stadiums, transit systems, and tech campuses. Growth: You will grow into a recognized subject matter expert in VDC delivery by managing large-scale coordination projects, engaging directly with clients, and expanding your technical proficiency across multiple software platforms. Motivators: You will thrive in a dynamic, entrepreneurial environment that values initiative, creativity, and knowledge sharing.
