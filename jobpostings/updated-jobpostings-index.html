üìö About Performance-based Hiring

Performance-based Hiring focuses on what candidates need to accomplish in the role rather than just listing skills and requirements. This approach:

  • Attracts stronger, more motivated candidates
  • Improves quality of hire metrics
  • Reduces time-to-fill positions
  • Creates more objective hiring decisions
